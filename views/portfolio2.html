Welcome to my project site

I am an Undergraduate from the Singapore University of Technology and Design (SUTD) pursuing a Computer Science degree in the Artificial Intelligence and Data Analytics Track with a minor in Digital Humanities and Health Informatics. My interest in applied ML research has led me to participate in several healthcare-related AI/ML projects through SUTD's Undergraduate Research Opportunity Programmes (UROP).



When I am not busy tackling data science challenges, I like to play the piano, keep up to date on the latest internet pop-culture trends and watch interesting scientific and historical documentaries about topics that are completely out of my depth.



This site showcases projects done thus far in my path towards achieving proficiency in Technology and Design.
